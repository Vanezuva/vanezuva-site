AIRWAVES: Ripple Effects hybrid media project

AIRWAVES: Ripple Effects is a hybrid media project examining how information moves, fractures, and accumulates meaning within digital systems shaped by power, attention, and control.

The work unfolds through a series of digital pieces, each reflecting the instability of truth within algorithmic environments where visibility is uneven and narratives are reshaped by invisible mechanisms. Repetition replaces understanding, absence becomes as telling as presence, and gaps emerge in transmission.

Collector Access & Participation

Collectors who mint an AIRWAVES edition gain entry to the broader project, including access to the submission portal linked below, where they may contribute media. These contributions become source material for immersive installations and an extended film. Over time, the work accumulates as a shared record built from many perspectives.
